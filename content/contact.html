Contact

In the comment section, briefly describe your musical abilities and goals. Request your day and time for group and/or individual music lessons.

Lessons are delivered over Zoom, Skype or Google Hangouts. Students must have a stable Internet connection. If the first lesson is satisfactory, then subsequent lessons may be paid for via PayPal or check.

If interested in this summer's Music Video camp, select "Music Video" from the dropdown "Other Interests". (details at MakerTeens.com/musicvideo/

Howdy Band does not sell or spam your email address. It is only used to give you information about the possibility of the lessons you request.
